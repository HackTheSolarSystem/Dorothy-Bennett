Scientific Work

Dorothy Bennett was known as an author for teenage books, and an illustrator and editor for
children's books. She worked with the Little Golden Books company mainly helping the authors
edit their books.

Throughout the years Bennett also took time to travel the wonders of the world, one of those wonders included traveling to Peru.
